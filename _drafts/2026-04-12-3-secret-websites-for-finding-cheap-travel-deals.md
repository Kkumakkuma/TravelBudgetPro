## Why Finding Cheap Travel Deals is Essential

Traveling can quickly become an expense-heavy experience if you're not careful. On average, a budget traveler might spend around $50 to $100 per day. This includes accommodation, meals, transport, and activities. But what if you could shave off 20% or even 30% from that total? Finding cheap travel deals not only helps you stay within your budget, but it also allows you to experience more!

## How to Use These Secret Websites

Before diving into the specifics, let's talk about how to use these websites effectively. 

1. **Be Flexible with Your Dates**: Most travel sites allow you to search with flexibility in mind. If you're open-minded about when you travel, you can find drastically lower prices.
  
2. **Set Price Alerts**: Many of the mentioned websites let you set alerts for specific routes or accommodations, meaning you'll be notified when prices drop.

3. **Combine Deals**: Don't hesitate to use multiple sites to ensure you're getting the best deal. Often, stacking up flight deals from one site with accommodation from another can yield incredible savings.

Now, let’s get into the nitty-gritty of these three secret travel websites.

## 1. Skyscanner

### What It Is

Skyscanner is a powerful flight comparison tool that allows you to search for flights across multiple airlines all in one place. However, it’s not just about flights; you can also find hotels and car rentals. 

### Why It’s Great for Budget Travelers

- **Flexible Search Options**: You can search for “Everywhere” as a destination if you have open travel dates, giving you a comprehensive overview of the cheapest places to fly.
  
- **Price Alerts**: Set up alerts to receive notifications about price drops for your chosen routes.

- **Hidden City Ticketing**: This little known tip can save serious cash. Skyscanner shows options for flights that may require layovers. For example, if you want to fly to Los Angeles from New York, check the price for a flight to a cheaper destination with a layover in Los Angeles. Sometimes, it’s significantly cheaper!

### Cost-Saving Examples

- I once found a round-trip flight from New York to Mexico City for just $200 during the off-season. That's over $100 cheaper than direct round-trips.

### How to Use It

Visit Skyscanner’s website or download their app. Simply enter your departure city, and if you’re flexible on destinations, choose “Everywhere.” You can then filter by price, duration, and more.

## 2. Hostelworld

### What It Is

Hostelworld is an excellent platform dedicated to budget accommodations, specifically hostels. It connects you with a plethora of options that are perfect for travelers looking to save money while socializing. 

### Benefits for Travelers

- **Wide Range of Budget Options**: Most hostels range from $10 to $40 per night. You can find even cheaper beds depending on the city and season.

- **User Reviews**: Every listing has user-generated reviews so you can choose a quality spot based on real experiences.

- **Meet Fellow Travelers**: Hostels often arrange meet-ups or social activities, perfect for solo travelers looking to connect.

### Examples to Illustrate

When traveling Southeast Asia, I booked a shared room in Bangkok for just $8 a night through Hostelworld. That’s less than $250 for 31 nights! Plus, the hostel had great amenities like free breakfast and tours which made my stay even more budget-friendly.

### How to Use It

Simply go to the Hostelworld website or app. Input your destination and travel dates. You can filter results by price, type, or amenities to find your perfect budget spot.

## 3. SecretFlying

### What It Is

SecretFlying is a lesser-known website that focuses on mistake fares and deals. It compiles incredible flight deals that often originate from pricing errors or promotional offers.

### Why It's a Game Changer

- **Mistake Fares**: Sometimes, airlines mistakenly publish fares at significantly lower prices. Subscribing to SecretFlying’s newsletter can mean you’re the first to know about these steals.

- **International Destinations**: SecretFlying often features deals that fly out of major U.S. cities to international destinations, some as low as $99 one-way!

### Real-Life Savings

For example, I once caught a mistake fare through SecretFlying, booking a round-trip flight from New York to Barcelona for just $300! Typically, these tickets range from $600 to $800. That’s the kind of savings that opens up traveling possibilities. 

### How to Use It

Check SecretFlying's website or social media pages regularly for the latest deals. Signing up for alerts can ensure you never miss out!

## Additional Money-Saving Tips 

While Skyscanner, Hostelworld, and SecretFlying will help you find cheap travel deals, here are some more strategies to keep your travel budget in check:

1. **Travel Off-Peak**: Traveling in the shoulder season (just before or after peak tourist months) can yield prices 20% lower than peak season.
  
2. **Public Transport**: Instead of taxis or rented cars, use buses or trains to get around. For instance, a bus ticket in Europe could save you over $20 compared to taking a taxi.

3. **Cook Your Meals**: Accommodation like hostels often provide kitchen facilities. Preparing simple meals can save you around $20 a day compared to dining out for every meal.

4. **Free Walking Tours**: Many cities offer free walking tours where you pay what you feel is fair. This is a great way to see the city and learn from locals.

5. **Local SIM Cards**: If heading abroad, buy a local SIM card for your phone instead of relying on pricey international roaming plans.

## Safety Tips for Travelers

Though traveling on a budget is all about saving, safety shouldn’t be compromised. Here are some basic safety tips:

- **Always Keep Your Belongings Secure**: Use anti-theft bags and keep your valuables close when in crowded places.

- **Share Your Itinerary**: Always let friends or family know your travel plans, especially if you're heading into less familiar territories.

- **Use Reputable Websites**: Stick with well-reviewed sites and apps for booking your travel to avoid scams.
