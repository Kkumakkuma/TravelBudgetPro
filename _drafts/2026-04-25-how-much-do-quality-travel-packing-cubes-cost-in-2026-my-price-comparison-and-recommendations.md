## Understanding the Basics of Packing Cubes

### What Are Packing Cubes?

Packing cubes are small fabric containers designed to help organize your luggage. They come in various sizes and shapes, allowing you to segregate items for easier access. I often use larger cubes for clothes and smaller ones for tech gadgets and toiletries.

- **Space Savers**: Packing cubes can compress clothing, allowing you to fit more into your luggage.
- **Organization**: Different cubes can be used for different types of items (e.g., tops, bottoms, shoes).

### Why You Need Packing Cubes

In my experience, packing cubes can drastically change how you pack. Here are a few reasons why they are essential for travelers:

1. **Efficiency**: Quickly locate your items without digging through a chaotic suitcase.
2. **Cleanliness**: Keep your clothing fresh and avoid the musty smell of an unorganized bag.
3. **Travel-Friendly**: Many cubes fit perfectly in carry-ons, making TSA checks hassle-free.

### Compression vs. Standard Packing Cubes

In my comparison, compression packing cubes tend to be about $10 more expensive than standard ones. They are typically designed to squeeze out excess air and help save even more space in your luggage.
