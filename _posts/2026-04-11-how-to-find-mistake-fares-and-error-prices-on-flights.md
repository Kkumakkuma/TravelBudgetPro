## What are Mistake Fares?

Mistake fares occur when airlines accidentally publish incorrect prices for flights. This can happen for a variety of reasons, such as software glitches, incorrect currency conversions, or human error. Regardless of the cause, these fares are usually significantly lower than the standard price, making them a hot ticket for budget travelers.

For instance, suppose you find a round-trip flight from New York to London for just $200 instead of the typical $600. You’d want to jump on that deal quickly, as mistake fares often don't last long!

## Why Mistake Fares Happen

Understanding why mistake fares occur can help you predict when to look for them. Some common reasons include:

- **System Errors**: Airlines regularly update their pricing systems, and these updates sometimes lead to incorrect fare listings.
- **Currency Fluctuations**: An airline may post a fare based on an incorrect currency conversion, accidentally leading to a lower price.
- **Human Errors**: Whether it’s a typing error during fare creation or setting the wrong season for travel dates, human errors crop up frequently.

Knowing this can remind you to stay vigilant and act swiftly when you stumble upon a deal that seems too good to be true. 

## How to Find Mistake Fares

Now that you understand what mistake fares are and why they occur, how do you find them? Below are several actionable strategies to help you snag those elusive deals:

### Subscribe to Fare Alerts

One of the easiest ways to stay in the loop is by subscribing to fare alert services. Here are some reputable websites that offer this service:

- **Scott's Cheap Flights**: Offers premium and free membership options with alerts on mistake fares and cheap flights. The premium option typically costs around $49 per year.
- **Airfarewatchdog**: Sends email alerts for mistake fares and deals tailored to your preferences.
- **Kayak**: Use their price alert feature to stay updated on routes you're interested in.

### Use Flight Aggregators and Search Engines

Flight search engines can help you to comb through numerous airlines and booking platforms simultaneously. Here are a few must-try options:

- **Google Flights**: Use the map feature to find the best deals within your desired region.
- **Skyscanner**: Allows you to search by "Everywhere," giving you the chance to see if there are any mistake fares from your departure city.
- **Momondo**: Known for transparent pricing, it helps you find a range of flights across various dates.

### Follow Travel Blogs and Forums

Travel bloggers and forums are great resources for insider information on mistake fares. Consider joining the following:

- **Flyertalk**: A community where travelers share the latest fare mistakes.
- **Travel blogs**: Keep an eye on reputable travel blogs, as experienced travelers often share errant fares when they spot them.

### Utilize Social Media

Don’t underestimate the power of social media. Following relevant accounts can lead you to some astonishing deals.

- **Twitter**: Follow accounts like @TheFlightDeal and @SecretFlying for real-time fare alerts.
- **Facebook Groups**: Join travel deal groups where members share mistake fares and discount flights.

## Apps to Monitor Mistake Fares

In addition to websites, several apps can help you monitor airfare deals on-the-go:

- **Hopper**: This app analyzes and predicts flight prices, notifying you when to book.
- **Kayak**: The app features fare alerts similar to its website counterpart.
- **AirfareWatchdog**: A straightforward app providing alerts for flight deals and mistake fares.

### The Importance of Flexibility

One of the most crucial aspects of successfully booking mistake fares is flexibility. Here are a few ways to enhance your flexibility:

1. **Travel Dates**: Often, mistake fares are available only on specific dates. If you can adjust your travel schedule, you increase your chances of finding a deal.
  
2. **Airports**: Be open to departing or arriving at alternative airports. Sometimes flying into a nearby city can save you a significant amount.

3. **Destinations**: Consider destinations that you might not have initially thought of. The "Explore" feature on Google Flights can help you find cheap fares to unexpected places.

## Things to Keep in Mind When Booking

Finding a mistake fare is just the first step; booking it also requires some careful strategy. Here are some useful tips:

### Act Fast

Mistake fares can disappear in mere hours, so when you find one that fits your travel plans, don't hesitate. The longer you wait, the higher the chances that the fare will be corrected.

### Book Directly with the Airline

After finding a mistake fare on a third-party platform, double-check whether you can book directly from the airline's website. Sometimes, airlines may honor the fare even after correcting a mistake. It’s worth a shot!

### Be Prepared for Changes

Mistake fares could lead to flight cancellations or significant changes, so always read the fine print before booking. For flights booked through third-party sites, the airline might not have the same flexibility with cancellations or changes.

### Purchase Travel Insurance

Especially when booking mistake fares, investing in travel insurance can provide additional peace of mind. Look for plans that cover cancellations, lost baggage, and other unforeseen events. Basic coverage can range from $20 to $50, which can be a small price for additional security.
